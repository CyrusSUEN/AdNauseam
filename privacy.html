AdNauseam Privacy Policy

We care about your privacy. AdNauseam does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you during its operation.


The AdNauseam log

AdNauseam writes some data to a log file in the Firefox profile during its operation. While this file is only stored locally and never transmitted by AdNauseam, other extensions and services (such as Firefox Sync) may attempt to do so. Most of this data is unobjectionable privacy-wise, however, the log could be used to reconstruct parts of your browsing history. To completely disable all AdNauseam logging, select "DISABLE ALL LOGGING" in the settings menu.


Extension update checks

Your browser periodically checks for updates of all of your extensions, including AdNauseam. General information, such as browser version, extension version, operating system and your IP address, are transmitted during such update checks.


'Private Browsing' mode

AdNauseam is automatically disabled whenever you enter 'Private Browsing' mode.
